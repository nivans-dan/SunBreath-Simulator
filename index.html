UV - A

UVA radiation, with a wavelength between 320 nm and 400 nm, constitutes 95% of the ultraviolet radiation that reaches the earth's surface and therefore our skin, this is the strongest radiation.

UV - B
Waves

View More

UV - B

The remaining 5% of the ultraviolet radiation that reaches the Earth is made up of UVB rays, with a wavelength between 280 nm and 320 nm.

UV - C
Waves

View More

UV - C

Its wavelength ranges between 200 and 280 nm. These rays are absorbed by the ozone layer before reaching the earth.

Info

Rubber Material

Polymer with elastic properties, it can deform considerably and return to its initial shape when the load to which it is subjected ceases. It can be produced synthetically or with latex obtained from various tropical plants.

PVC

Info

PVC Material

It is a chemical combination of carbon, hydrogen and chlorine. Its components come from crude oil (43%) and salt (57%). It is the plastic with the least dependence on oil.

Polyethersulfone - PES

Info

Polyethersulfone Material

High temperature resistant engineering thermoplastic with excellent temperature tolerance over extended periods. PES polymer offers high mechanical strength and stiffness, as well as relatively low indentation sensitivity.

Polystyrene

Info

Polystyrene Material

Versatile plastic used to make a wide variety of consumer products. Since it is a hard, solid plastic, it is frequently used in products that require transparency, such as food containers and laboratory equipment.

Silk

Info

Design for tecnology & services

Natural fiber of animal origin constituted in very fine continuous monofilament, with which luxury and high value articles are manufactured thanks to its natural shine, good drape, good absorption of humidity and dry, soft and pleasant touch.

Polycaprolactone - PCL

Info

Polycaprolactone Material

It finds applications in adhesives, compatibilizing agents and films as well as in medicine. The most common use of polycaprolactone as an additive is in the manufacture of special polyurethanes.
